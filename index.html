Research

I have undertaken extensive research regarding alternative thickeners in hand cream formulations as part of my professional endeavors.

To goals of this study were to substitute guar gum in a hand cream with xanthan gum, hydropropyl methyl cellulose, ceratonia siliqua (carob) gum (and) sclerotium gum, carbomer, cellulose gum, gellan gum formulate the different versions of the hand cream. Second goal, evaluating the changes that occurred via visual and instrumental techniques. The third goal was to finetune the best hand cream containing one of the alternative thickeners to closely match the original hand cream.
