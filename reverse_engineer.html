Project Summary

Reverse engineering project is make a product base on the original product by keeping or substituting in ingredients list. I was learning about how the order of cosmetic was arranged, find ingredients that I could replace by using our company ingredient like preservative, shea butter… I also distinguished what kind of phase for each ingredient to prepare formulating process. After every time formulating, I will write a report what I observe, feel, and give some adjustment if need. After each trial, I was learning about how the impact of ingredient as well as all the formulation. When finishing a reverse engineering project, I keep up with stability testing that last long 10 weeks to see that my project is passed or fail or need to be researched more. I think this project is helpful for my ingredient’s knowledge, I had the chance to work with many ingredients and know them better. Furthermore, the project will help me to have base knowledge if I want to be a formulator or working in manufacturing in the future.
